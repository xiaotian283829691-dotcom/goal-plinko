# Asset: Game Board Background

## Usage
Full-screen background texture behind the Plinko pin board. Must not distract from gameplay — subtle, dark, atmospheric. Visible on mobile screens (primarily 390x844 viewport).

## Image Spec
- **Size**: 1080x1920 px (9:16 portrait), PNG or JPG
- **Quantity**: 1 image

## Prompt for GPT image2

> A dark atmospheric background texture for a mobile game. Top-down view of a football stadium grass field at night, heavily darkened with deep vignette edges. The grass texture is barely visible — just enough to hint at a stadium setting. Color palette: deep forest green (#0a1a0f) to near-black (#050a05). Subtle stadium floodlight glow from the top creating a faint gradient. No players, no lines, no markings — pure ambient texture. Portrait orientation 1080x1920 pixels.

## Style Notes
- This is a DARK background — the game board (pins, ball, slots) sits on top of it
- The grass hint should be extremely subtle, like looking at a pitch from above with lights dimmed to 10%
- Heavy vignette: edges should fade to near-black
- No bright spots that would compete with the gold UI elements
- Should tile seamlessly if the game area scrolls
