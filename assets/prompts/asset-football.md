# Asset: Football (Game Ball)

## Usage
The primary ball sprite in the Plinko game. Displayed at small size (12-24px diameter on screen) but needs to be crisp at 2x/3x retina. Will be rendered on a dark background.

## Image Spec
- **Size**: 128x128 px, PNG with transparent background
- **Quantity**: 1 image

## Prompt for GPT image2

> A classic soccer ball icon, 3D rendered style with soft lighting from top-left. Traditional black and white hexagonal/pentagonal pattern. Slight glossy highlight on the upper-left surface. Clean edges, no shadow, transparent background. The ball should look like a premium game asset — crisp, modern, with subtle depth. Render at 128x128 pixels.

## Style Notes
- Must read clearly at 24px display size — avoid overly detailed textures
- The black pentagon pattern should be clearly visible even when small
- Slight 3D depth but not overly realistic — stylized game asset feel
- No ground shadow (will be composited onto game board)
